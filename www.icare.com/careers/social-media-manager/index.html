Social Media Manager

  • Careers
  • Details

Job Description

We are looking for a person  who is passionate about marketing and would like to manage multiple social media platforms such as Linkedin, Facebook, Quora, Pinterest and Instagram. This is a great opportunity for an experienced social media enthusiast with a passion for storytelling, community building and creating engaging content. This role includes high ownership and accountability.

Responsibilities

  • Understand the competitive landscape from the standpoint of strategy and functional capabilities.
  • Establish best practices and processes for Storytelling about Product, Brand, and Clients through the social media pages.
  • Create original content and work closely with the graphic team to create in-trend content on pages.
  • Ensure the brand message reaches the relevant audience at the relevant time through various organic and paid campaigns on various social media platforms.
  • Engaging through comments, DMS, story, reels etc.
  • Using analytics and insights to track and report on performance, and continually optimise strategy and delivery.

Requirements

  • A strong editorial sense and an understanding of what content works on social media platforms.
  • Excellent writing skills and creative flair across copy, imagery and GIF selection.
  • Strong written communication and accuracy Experience in building and engaging existing communities as well as reaching out and attracting new ones.
  • Experience of using social media analytics to provide evaluation and ideas for solutions, new projects and the bigger picture
  • Confidence in presenting new ideas and strategies with an experience of, or an interest in handling influencer relationships from research to outreach and gifting.
